Our Commitment to Your Privacy
At SkillSift, we prioritize and respect your privacy. We want to assure you that we take the utmost care in handling your sensitive information.

Resume Processing:
When you upload your resume, rest assured that we do not store it on our servers. Our platform processes your resume in real-time, extracting relevant skills to provide you with accurate insights. Once the analysis is complete, your resume is immediately removed from our system.

Secure Handling:
We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard your data during the entire process. Our commitment is to ensure the confidentiality and privacy of your information.

No Account Required:
SkillSift is designed with simplicity in mind. We do not require users to create accounts, eliminating the need for unnecessary data storage. Your experience with SkillSift is hassle-free and focused on delivering valuable insights without compromising your privacy.
